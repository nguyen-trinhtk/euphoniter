EUPHONITER - THE START OF A JOURNEY



Hi there! Welcome to my wonderland of tunes and scribbles. Here, you can find posts written by me about the fascinating world of music on four main topics: music history, theory, instruments, and miscellaneous. Hopefully, you can find your "harmony" and discover new aspects.


About me

Ddun durun dun dun! It's me, the person behind this blog.


My name is Khanh Nguyen. I am currently a high schooler based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.


​

Having a special interest in music, history, and science, I want to share my knowledge and enthusiasm with my readers through this blog and inspire that musical friend inside you. 


A little note here: All the writings and images on this site are created by me. Please do not use them without my permission elsewhere.


Why "Euphoniter"?

Euphoniter is the fusion between two Latin-originated words "Euphony"- pleasing harmonies - and "Iter" - journey.


At Euphoniter, you can immerse in music your way through different stories that combine music with other areas such as history, science, or culture. 


Lots of fascinating experiences are awaiting you! Now, let's get right to discovering music. Have fun!
